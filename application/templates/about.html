What is EnComp?

EnComp is a platform that will enable engineers to digitalize calculations which would traditionally be done by hand or in custom spreadsheets.

EnComp not only hosts scalable and streamlined calculations but can help ensure your organization's young engineers are aware of the engineering checks and code references that go into a design. Additionally, the detailed calculation reports boost transparency during quality control and submittal reviews.

EnComp is an open-source platform and interested engineers are always welcome to collaborate on Github.


Vision

In an industry with no margin for error and where efficiency is king - automating calculations that are beyond the realms of Finite Element Analysis software are essential.

Our vision is to empower engineers to create customizable templates for frequently used calculations that allow for quick engineering design checks and automated reporting.
